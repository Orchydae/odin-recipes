Bun Bo Hue

Description

Bun Bo Hue is a spicy Vietnamese noodle soup that is a staple of Vietnamese cuisine.

It is a perfect blend of spicy, salty, and umami flavors.

It is a popular street food in Vietnam and the specialty of the city of Hue in central Vietnam from which it derives its name.

Ingredients

    Broth Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs beef bones
  • 1 lb beef shank
  • 1 lb pork hocks
  • 12 cups water

    • Broth Spices

  • 4 stalks lemongrass
  • 2 onions
  • 2 whole star anise

Directions

  1. In a large pot, add cold water into it and all the meats.
  2. Bring to a boil and boil for 5 minutes.
  3. Drain and rinse the bones and meat under running water.
